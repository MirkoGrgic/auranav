The Best Time to Visit Santorini

Avoid crowds, find better hotel prices, enjoy beautiful sunsets, and choose the perfect season for Greece’s most famous island.

6 min read Santorini Estimated Cost: $2,000

Why timing matters in Santorini

Santorini is beautiful year-round, but the experience can change dramatically depending on when you visit. Peak summer brings warm weather and lively energy, but also higher prices and heavy crowds.

Shoulder seasons offer a better balance: good weather, fewer people, and more relaxed sightseeing.

AuraNav tip: For the best mix of weather, prices, and crowds, visit Santorini in May, early June, September, or early October.

May and early June

Late spring is one of the best times to visit. The island feels fresh, restaurants and hotels are open, and crowds are still manageable. Temperatures are warm enough for exploring without the intensity of midsummer.

July and August

Summer is Santorini’s busiest season. Expect hot days, crowded viewpoints, expensive accommodation, and busy restaurants. It can still be magical, but it requires more planning and earlier reservations.

September and early October

Early autumn is another excellent period. The sea is still warm, sunsets are beautiful, and crowds begin to thin after peak summer.

Winter and low season

Winter is quiet and affordable, but many tourist-focused businesses may close. This season is better for travelers who want solitude and photography rather than beach days or nightlife.

Best time for sunsets

Santorini sunsets are beautiful throughout the year, but spring and autumn provide a more comfortable experience because viewpoints are less crowded.

Planning tips

  • Book accommodation early if visiting in July or August.
  • Stay outside Oia for better value.
  • Reserve sunset restaurants in advance.
  • Use buses or taxis carefully during peak season.
  • Visit popular viewpoints early in the morning.

Final Thoughts

The best time to visit Santorini depends on your travel style. For most travelers, shoulder season offers the best balance of beauty, comfort, and value.
