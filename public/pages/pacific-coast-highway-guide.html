Road Trips

California Pacific Coast Highway

Drive one of the most iconic coastal routes in the world, passing dramatic cliffs, ocean viewpoints, beach towns, and unforgettable California scenery.

9 min read California Estimated Cost: $2,600

America's most scenic coastal drive

The Pacific Coast Highway (Highway 1) stretches along California's coastline and delivers breathtaking ocean views, dramatic cliffs, charming seaside towns, and world-famous landmarks.

Whether you're driving the entire route or only exploring sections around Big Sur, the journey itself becomes the main attraction.

AuraNav tip: Drive north to south if possible. You'll have easier access to scenic roadside viewpoints along the ocean side.

Start in San Francisco

Begin your road trip in San Francisco. Visit the Golden Gate Bridge, Fisherman's Wharf, and enjoy one of California's most vibrant cities before heading south.

Big Sur

Big Sur is the highlight of the Pacific Coast Highway. Towering cliffs drop into the Pacific Ocean while winding roads reveal spectacular viewpoints around every corner.

Bixby Bridge

One of California's most photographed landmarks, Bixby Bridge offers incredible views and is a must-stop location during the drive.

Monterey and Carmel

These charming coastal towns combine beautiful beaches, seafood, art galleries, and relaxed California atmosphere.

Santa Barbara

Known for Mediterranean-style architecture and oceanfront scenery, Santa Barbara is one of the best overnight stops along the route.

End in Los Angeles

Finish your adventure in Los Angeles where beaches, entertainment, restaurants, and iconic attractions await.

Road Trip Tips

  • Allow at least 5–7 days for the full experience.
  • Book hotels early during summer.
  • Bring a camera for coastal viewpoints.
  • Check road conditions around Big Sur.
  • Plan extra stops—you'll want them.

Final Thoughts

The Pacific Coast Highway is more than a drive—it's one of the world's great road trip experiences. Every mile offers new scenery, unforgettable viewpoints, and classic California charm.
