Driving Across the Amalfi Coast

Experience one of Europe's most spectacular coastal drives, passing colorful cliffside villages, dramatic sea views, and unforgettable Italian scenery.

8 min read Italy Estimated Cost: $2,400

Italy's most famous coastal road

The Amalfi Coast is legendary among road-trip enthusiasts. The winding roads connect picturesque towns perched above the Mediterranean, offering breathtaking views around nearly every corner.

Although the route itself is relatively short, the journey is all about stopping frequently, exploring villages, and enjoying the scenery.

AuraNav tip: Start driving early in the morning to avoid heavy traffic and enjoy the best lighting for photography.

Start in Sorrento

Sorrento serves as the ideal gateway to the Amalfi Coast. Its historic center, ocean views, and ferry connections make it a great place to begin your journey.

Discover Positano

Positano is the Amalfi Coast's most iconic destination, famous for colorful buildings cascading down steep cliffs toward the sea.

Visit Amalfi Town

The town of Amalfi offers beautiful architecture, lively piazzas, and access to some of the region's most important historical landmarks.

Explore Ravello

Sitting high above the coast, Ravello provides panoramic views and elegant gardens that have inspired artists and travelers for generations.

Enjoy local cuisine

Fresh seafood, handmade pasta, local wines, and Amalfi lemons are essential parts of the experience.

Road trip tips

  • Book accommodations months in advance.
  • Drive cautiously on narrow cliffside roads.
  • Use smaller rental cars when possible.
  • Allow extra time for traffic during summer.
  • Carry cash for parking areas and small cafés.

Final Thoughts

The Amalfi Coast is less about covering distance and more about enjoying every stop along the way. With stunning views, charming towns, and incredible food, it remains one of the world's most rewarding road trips.
