Road Trips

The Scottish Highlands Adventure

Discover ancient castles, misty mountains, remote lochs, winding roads, and some of the most atmospheric landscapes anywhere in Europe.

7 min read Scotland Estimated Cost: $1,900

Why the Highlands belong on every road trip list

The Scottish Highlands combine dramatic scenery, medieval history, legendary castles, and some of the most beautiful driving roads in the United Kingdom.

Every route reveals towering mountains, quiet glens, remote villages, and breathtaking lochs that have inspired stories and legends for centuries.

AuraNav tip: Weather changes quickly in Scotland. Bring waterproof clothing even if the forecast looks perfect.

Begin in Inverness

Known as the capital of the Highlands, Inverness serves as the perfect starting point. Explore the city before heading into the surrounding wilderness.

Visit Loch Ness

One of Scotland's most famous locations, Loch Ness offers beautiful scenery, historic ruins, and of course the legendary Nessie stories that attract visitors from around the world.

Explore Glencoe

Glencoe is often considered the most spectacular valley in Scotland. Towering mountains and dramatic landscapes make it a favorite stop for hikers and photographers.

Drive the North Coast 500

The NC500 route has become one of Europe's most famous road trips, connecting remote beaches, fishing villages, mountain roads, and stunning coastal scenery.

Discover Historic Castles

Scotland's castles are everywhere. Eilean Donan Castle is among the most photographed, while dozens of smaller castles hide across the countryside.

Wildlife and Nature

Red deer, golden eagles, seals, and even dolphins can be spotted throughout different parts of the Highlands.

Road Trip Tips

  • Drive carefully on single-track roads.
  • Book accommodation early during summer.
  • Carry snacks for remote sections.
  • Expect changing weather conditions.
  • Allow time for spontaneous photo stops.

Final Thoughts

The Scottish Highlands deliver a perfect combination of history, scenery, and adventure. Whether you're exploring castles or chasing mountain views, every day feels like a journey through a storybook.
